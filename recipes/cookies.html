Shortbread Cookies

Description

These shortbread cookies are a classic treat that never goes out of style. With their buttery flavor and crumbly texture, they are the perfect accompaniment to a cup of tea or coffee. Whether you enjoy them plain or dressed up with a drizzle of chocolate or a sprinkle of sugar, these cookies are sure to satisfy your sweet tooth.

Ingredients

  • 1 Cup Butter, Softened
  • 1/2 Cup Powdered Sugar
  • 1/4 Teaspoon Salt
  • 2 Cups All-Purpose Flour

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C).
  2. In a medium bowl, cream together the butter, powdered sugar, and salt until light and fluffy.
  3. Gradually stir in the flour until well combined.
  4. Roll the dough out on a floured surface to 1/4 inch thickness and cut into desired shapes using cookie cutters.
  5. Place cookies 1 inch apart on an ungreased cookie sheet.
  6. Bake for 8 to 10 minutes, or until the edges are lightly golden.
  7. Cool on wire racks and enjoy!
